# 🚀 Project Overview
**Name**: ApplyandConnect
**Role**: full-stack / backend / AI engineer
**Type**: Career search platform

**Summary**: ApplyandConnect is an AI-powered job search operating system that combines job discovery, LLM-based resume tailoring, recruiter outreach, Gmail pipeline sync, and extension-assisted applications in one platform. It uses Gemini-based server-side workflows for job-description understanding, ATS-focused resume optimization, cover-letter generation, recruiter email drafting, contact discovery, and job-email classification. To make outputs more reliable, the resume pipeline lets the LLM rewrite only high-value dynamic sections while deterministically preserving the rest of the document.

# 🛠️ AI Platform Capabilities
- **Gemini AI Layer**: Centralized server-side Gemini workflows power JD parsing, resume tailoring, cover letters, recruiter email drafting, contact discovery, and job-email classification.
- **LLM-Safe Resume Pipeline**: Rewrites only dynamic resume sections while deterministically stitching static content back in to reduce hallucination and formatting drift.
- **Extension AI Workflows**: Supports direct profile tailoring, application-package generation, cover-letter generation, autofill, and application sync on LinkedIn and major job boards.
- **Agentic Orchestration**: Active work covers job shortlisting, outreach preparation, and LinkedIn automation, with a broader multi-agent architecture already documented.
- **Model Routing and Usage Controls**: Tracks model selection, token usage, and cost visibility across Gemini model tiers to support scaling and company-level controls.
- **Adoption and Operations**: Live product at applyandconnect.com with 10+ users in one company and expansion tied to measurable workflow outcomes.

# 🧠 AI-Focused Case Study

I built ApplyandConnect as an AI-first career platform designed to reduce the manual workload of job searching. The product uses server-side Gemini workflows for job-description understanding, ATS-focused resume tailoring, cover-letter generation, recruiter email drafting, contact discovery, and job-email classification.

To make the AI output more reliable, I implemented resume pipelines that only let the LLM rewrite high-value dynamic sections while deterministically preserving the rest of the document.

I also built extension-based AI workflows for LinkedIn and other job boards, including direct profile tailoring, application-package generation, autofill support, and application tracking back into the main platform.

In parallel, I’ve been working on agentic workflows for job selection, outreach, and LinkedIn automation, with a broader multi-agent architecture for shortlisting, resume strategy, follow-ups, cold outreach, and token auditing.

The platform is live at applyandconnect.com, currently used by 10+ users within one company, and positioned for expansion based on performance, operational efficiency, and hiring outcomes.
