🚀 Our Startup Story

DormUnity was born from a simple observation: college students were juggling multiple apps for basic campus needs. From finding rides to discovering accommodation, from splitting expenses to connecting with peers - everything was fragmented.

We envisioned a single, powerful platform that would become the central hub for campus life. After months of research, user interviews, and technical development, Dormigo emerged as the comprehensive solution students have been waiting for.
